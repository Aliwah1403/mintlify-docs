The Expenses section gives you a complete view of all money spent on your fleet operations. Every cost recorded in the Expenditure section flows here, organized and ready for analysis.

This is where you answer questions like: "How much did we spend on fuel this month?" or "What are our total repair costs across all vehicles?"

## What You Can See

The Expenses section is divided into three tabs:

- **All** - Complete view of every expense across all categories
- **Fuel** - Dedicated fuel cost tracking with discount management
- **Others** - Non-fuel expenses (clerk fees, mileage, parking, repairs, extras)

Each tab shows totals at the top right and allows filtering by date range.

## How Expenses Flow Into This Section

Expenses don't get entered here directly. Instead, they come from trips you record in the Expenditure section:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trip recorded in Expenditure">
    You enter a trip with fuel cost KES 8,000, parking KES 500, and repair cost KES 3,000
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Costs automatically categorized">
    Travada splits these amounts into their respective categories
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Expenses appear in this section">
    Fuel shows in the Fuel tab, parking and repairs show in Others tab, all show in All tab
  </Step>
</Steps>

This means the Expenses section is **read-only** — you view and analyze costs here, but you record them in Expenditure.

## The Three Expense Views

### All Expenses

Shows every cost across all categories in one combined view. Useful for:

- Getting total spend for a period
- Generating expense reports for management
- Seeing which trips had the highest costs
- Month-end reconciliation

### Fuel Expenses

Dedicated to fuel tracking because fuel is typically your largest expense category. This tab shows:

- Total fuel cost
- Total fuel discounts received
- Individual fuel entries per trip
- Net fuel spend after discounts

**Special feature:** You can add fuel discounts in bulk here, which is covered in detail in [Fuel Discounts](/features/expenses/fuel-discounts).

### Other Expenses

Groups all non-fuel costs together:

- **Clerk fees** - Depot payments for container clearance
- **Milage fees** - Long-distance driver allowances
- **Parking fees** - Parking charges
- **Repair costs** - Garage and maintenance expenses
- **Extra costs** - Car wash, traffic fines, etc.

Shows the combined total of these categories.

## Key Difference: Expenses vs Expenditure

This confuses new users, so let's clarify:

| Section | Purpose | What You Do |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Expenditure** | Trip entry | Record trips with all costs |
| **Expenses** | Cost analysis | View and filter costs already recorded |

Think of it this way:
- **Expenditure** = Input (where costs go in)
- **Expenses** = Output (where costs are analyzed)

## Who Can Use This Section

Both Admin and Staff users can view expenses. The only restriction:

<Note>
**🔒 Admin Only** - Generating expense reports and adding fuel discounts in bulk requires Admin privileges.
</Note>

Staff users can view all expense data and filter it, but cannot create reports or apply discounts.

## Common Use Cases

**Daily Operations Manager:**
"Let me check today's fuel spend across all vehicles"
→ Go to Fuel tab, filter to today's date

**Finance Team (Weekly):**
"I need all expenses for last week to reconcile with bank statements"
→ Go to All tab, set date range to last week, generate expense report

**Fleet Manager (Monthly):**
"Which expense category is growing fastest?"
→ Compare monthly totals in All tab across different periods

**Owner (Strategic):**
"Are repair costs increasing? Should we replace old vehicles?"
→ Filter Others tab by repair cost, compare across months

## What You Can Do Here

From the Expenses section, you can:

- **View all costs** organized by category
- **Filter by date range** to analyze specific periods
- **See totals** automatically calculated
- **Generate expense reports** for management or accounting (Admin only)
- **Track fuel discounts** and see actual savings
- **Export data** for external analysis
- **Identify cost trends** over time

## Integration with Other Sections

Expenses data connects to:

- **Dashboard** - The Expense Breakdown chart pulls from here
- **Expenditure** - This is the source of all expense data
- **Reports** - Expense reports are generated from this data
- **Vehicle Performance** - Per-vehicle expense totals feed vehicle profitability calculations
