Account Settings is where you manage your personal information, security preferences, and Travada account details. This section is separate from workspace settings - changes here affect only your individual account.

## Your Account Profile

Your Travada account consists of several key pieces of information:

### Basic Information

- **Full Name** - Your display name across the platform
- **Email Address** - Used for login and notifications
- **Phone Number** - Contact information (optional)
- **User Role** - Admin or Staff (set by account owner)
- **Company/Organization** - Your business name

### Account Status

- **Account Type** - Individual or Team account
- **Subscription Status** - Active, trial, or expired
- **Member Since** - When your account was created
- **Last Login** - Most recent access time

## What You Can Edit

Different account fields have different editability:

| Field | Can Edit | How to Change |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| Full Name | ✅ Yes | Directly in Account Settings |
| Email Address | ✅ Yes | Requires verification |
| Phone Number | ✅ Yes | Directly in Account Settings |
| Password | ✅ Yes | Security section |
| User Role | ❌ No | Contact account owner/admin |
| Company Name | ❌ No | Contact support |

## Changing Your Email Address

Your email is critical for login and notifications, so changing it requires verification:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account Settings">
    Click the gear icon and select **Account**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Edit Email">
    Find your current email and click the **Edit** button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter new email">
    Type your new email address carefully
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify the change">
    Check both your old and new email for verification links
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm in both inboxes">
    Click the verification link in your new email
    Acknowledge the change notice in your old email
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**Important:** Until you verify the new email, your account continues using the old address. Don't delete your old email account until verification is complete.
</Warning>

### Why Email Verification Matters

Email verification protects you from:

- Accidental typos that lock you out
- Unauthorized email changes by others
- Loss of access to critical notifications
- Account security breaches

## Password Management

### Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Security">
    In Account Settings, find the **Security** section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Change Password">
    Select the **Change Password** option
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter current password">
    Verify your identity with your current password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter new password">
    Type your new password twice to confirm
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Update Password** to save
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Requirements

Travada requires passwords to be:

- Minimum 8 characters long
- Include at least one uppercase letter
- Include at least one number
- Include at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

<Tip>
**Strong Password Tips:**
- Use a phrase with numbers: "MyFleet2026!"
- Avoid common words like "password" or "admin"
- Don't reuse passwords from other accounts
- Consider using a password manager
</Tip>

### If You Forgot Your Password

Can't remember your password? Use the password reset flow:

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

The reset link expires after 24 hours for security.

## Understanding User Roles

Your user role determines what you can do in Travada:

### Admin Role

Full system access including:

- Add/edit/delete vehicles and drivers
- Generate all types of reports
- Apply fuel discounts
- Access all settings
- Manage team members (on Team plans)
- View all financial data

### Staff Role

Limited access focused on operations:

- Record trips in Expenditure
- View data and dashboards
- Filter and export data
- Cannot add/edit/delete vehicles or drivers
- Cannot generate reports
- Cannot access workspace settings

<Info>
**Can't change your own role** - Only the account owner or another Admin can change your role. If you need Admin access, contact your company's account owner.
</Info>

## Account Security Best Practices

Protect your Travada account by following these guidelines:

### Regular Security Checks

**Monthly:**
- Review recent login activity (if available)
- Ensure email address is current
- Check notification settings are working

**Quarterly:**
- Change your password
- Review user roles (for Admins managing teams)
- Update phone number if changed

### Signs of Unauthorized Access

Contact support immediately if you notice:

- Emails about login attempts you didn't make
- Password reset emails you didn't request
- Trips or vehicles you didn't create
- Reports you didn't generate
- Email address changed without your knowledge

### Secure Login Habits

<Tip>
**Security Checklist:**
- ✅ Use a unique password (not shared with other accounts)
- ✅ Log out when using shared computers
- ✅ Don't share your login credentials
- ✅ Enable email notifications for account changes
- ✅ Keep your registered email secure
</Tip>

## Session Management

Travada handles sessions to balance security and convenience:

- **Active session:** 24 hours of continuous activity
- **Inactivity timeout:** 2 hours of no activity = auto logout
- **Multi-device:** Can be logged in on multiple devices simultaneously
- **Logout:** Manually log out to end session immediately

If you're logged out unexpectedly, it's likely due to the inactivity timeout.

## Account Deletion

Want to delete your Travada account? Here's what to know:

<Warning>
**Permanent Action** - Account deletion removes all your data permanently and cannot be undone. Consider disabling your account temporarily instead.
</Warning>

### What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account:

- All personal information
- All trip records you created
- All reports you generated
- Your login credentials
- Notification preferences
- All historical data

### What Happens to Team Data

If you're part of a team account:

- Your personal data is removed
- Trips/vehicles/drivers you created may remain (company data)
- Other team members retain their access
- Company subscription continues

### How to Request Deletion

Account deletion requires contacting support:

1. Email support@travadasys.com
2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
3. Include your registered email and company name
4. Confirm you understand data will be permanently lost
5. Support will process within 48 hours

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account vs Workspace Settings

New users often confuse these:

| Setting Type | Account Settings | Workspace Settings |
|--------------|------------------|-------------------|
| **Scope** | Personal (only you) | Team-wide (everyone) |
| **Examples** | Email, password, personal notifications | Vehicles, drivers, appearance |
| **Who can change** | You | Admin only |
| **Effects** | Only your experience | All team members |

**Simple rule:** If it's about YOU personally (email, password, your notifications), it's Account Settings. If it's about the BUSINESS (vehicles, drivers, theme), it's Workspace Settings.
