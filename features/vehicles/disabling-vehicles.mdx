## Overview

Disabling vehicles is how you remove them from active use in Travada while preserving all historical data. This is the recommended approach when vehicles are sold, contracts end, or vehicles go out of service.

<img src='/images/disabled-vehicles-page.png' alt='Disabled Vehicles Page' />

## Disable vs. Delete

Understanding the critical difference:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title='✅ Disable (Recommended)'>
    **What happens:** - Vehicle removed from trip dropdowns - All historical
    data preserved - Can be re-enabled later - Still appears in historical
    reports - Performance data intact **Use when:** - Vehicle sold - Contract
    ended (hired vehicles) - Long-term repairs - Seasonal inactive period - Any
    time vehicle won't be used for trips
  </Tab>

  <Tab title='❌ Delete (Not Recommended)'>
    **What happens:** - Vehicle completely removed - All trip history lost -
    Income/expense data orphaned - Reports may break - Cannot be undone **Use
    when:** - Almost never! - Only for test vehicles with no trip data - Only
    for duplicate entries never used
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  **Always Disable, Never Delete!** Deleting vehicles destroys historical data
  and can corrupt your financial records. Disable instead.
</Warning>

## When to Disable Vehicles

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title='Vehicle Sold' icon='handshake'>
    **Scenario:** Sold one of your owned vehicles **Action:** Disable
    immediately after sale **Reason:** - No longer available for trips -
    Historical data needed for accounting - May need for tax purposes **Note:**
    Keep disabled until end of financial year, then review
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title='Hire Contract Ended' icon='file-contract'>
  **Scenario:** Lease/rental period finished **Action:** Disable when contract
  expires **Reason:** - Vehicle returned to owner - Historical trip data needed
  for billing verification - Contract performance review **Tip:** Set calendar
  reminder for contract end dates
</Accordion>

<Accordion title='Major Repairs/Overhaul' icon='wrench'>
  **Scenario:** Vehicle in workshop for extended period **Action:** Disable
  while under repair **Reason:** - Prevents accidental trip assignment - Clearly
  shows vehicle unavailable - Can re-enable when repairs complete **Timeline:**
  2+ weeks out of service
</Accordion>

<Accordion title='Seasonal Operations' icon='calendar'>
  **Scenario:** Vehicle only used certain times of year **Action:** Disable
  during off-season **Reason:** - Cleaner active vehicle list - Focuses team on
  operational fleet - Easy to re-enable when needed **Example:** School vans
  disabled during holidays
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Permanent Retirement' icon='trash'>
    **Scenario:** Vehicle scrapped or permanently retired **Action:** Disable
    permanently **Reason:** - Historical data preserved - Financial records
    complete - Fleet history maintained **Note:** Will never re-enable, but keep
    in system
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Disable a Vehicle

### Single Vehicle

<Steps>
  <Step title='Navigate to Disabled Vehicles'>
    Click **Vehicles** → **Disabled Vehicles** in sidebar
    <img
      src='/images/disabled-vehicles-nav.png'
      alt='Disabled Vehicles Navigation'
    />
  </Step>

<Step title='Click Disable Vehicle Button'>
  Click **+ Disable Vehicle** button (top right)
  <img src='/images/disable-vehicle-button.png' alt='Disable Vehicle Button' />
</Step>

<Step title='Select Vehicle'>
  A dialog opens with checkboxes for all active vehicles Check the box next to
  the vehicle(s) you want to disable
  <img src='/images/disable-vehicle-dialog.png' alt='Disable Vehicle Dialog' />
</Step>

<Step title='Confirm Disable'>
  Click **Disable** or **Confirm** Success message appears
</Step>

  <Step title='Verify'>
    - Vehicle disappears from Add/Manage Vehicles list - Vehicle appears in
    Disabled Vehicles list - No longer in trip dropdown menus
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Multiple Vehicles

You can disable several vehicles at once:

<Steps>
  <Step title='Open Disable Dialog'>
    Navigate to Disabled Vehicles → Click **+ Disable Vehicle**
  </Step>

<Step title='Select Multiple'>
  Check boxes for all vehicles you want to disable **Use cases:** - All vehicles
  from ended contract - Entire category (e.g., all school vans for summer) -
  Multiple sold vehicles
</Step>

  <Step title='Confirm'>
    Click **Disable** All selected vehicles disabled at once
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Batch Disabling:** If ending a contract with multiple hired vehicles,
  disable them all together for efficiency.
</Tip>

## What Happens When You Disable

Immediate effects:

✅ **Removed from:**

- Trip recording dropdowns
- Active vehicle lists
- Future trip assignments

✅ **Preserved in:**

- Historical trip data
- Dashboard analytics (historical)
- Generated reports
- Expense and income records

✅ **Visible in:**

- Disabled Vehicles page
- Historical performance data
- Past period reports

<Info>
  Disabled vehicles are like "archived" - they exist in the system but are
  marked as inactive.
</Info>

## Re-enabling Vehicles

If you need to bring a vehicle back into service:

<Steps>
  <Step title='Go to Disabled Vehicles'>
    Navigate to **Vehicles** → **Disabled Vehicles**
  </Step>

<Step title='Find the Vehicle'>Locate the disabled vehicle in the list</Step>

<Step title='Click Enable'>
  Click the **Enable** button on the vehicle card
  <img src='/images/enable-vehicle-button.png' alt='Enable Vehicle Button' />
  Or click **⋮** → **Enable** from menu
</Step>

<Step title='Confirm'>Confirm you want to re-enable</Step>

  <Step title='Verify'>
    - Vehicle moves back to Add/Manage Vehicles list - Disappears from Disabled
    Vehicles - Available in trip dropdowns again - All historical data intact
  </Step>
</Steps>

### When to Re-enable

<Tabs>
  <Tab title='Repairs Complete'>
    **Scenario:** Vehicle returned from workshop **Action:** Re-enable to resume
    operations **Verify:** Test drive completed, roadworthy
  </Tab>

<Tab title='Season Starts'>
  **Scenario:** School year begins, tours resume **Action:** Re-enable seasonal
  vehicles **Verify:** Maintenance up-to-date, ready for service
</Tab>

<Tab title='Contract Renewed'>
  **Scenario:** Hire contract extended **Action:** Re-enable previously disabled
  hired vehicle **Update:** Verify ownership status still correct
</Tab>

  <Tab title='Error Correction'>
    **Scenario:** Disabled wrong vehicle by mistake **Action:** Immediately
    re-enable **Note:** Check trips weren't assigned to wrong vehicle meanwhile
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Deleting Disabled Vehicles

<Warning>**Danger Zone:** Deleting is permanent and destroys all data!</Warning>

You _can_ delete vehicles from the Disabled Vehicles page, but **only do this if:**

✅ Test vehicle with zero trips  
✅ Duplicate entry never used  
✅ Created by mistake with no data

❌ **Never delete if:**

- Has any trip history
- Has income or expense records
- Appeared in any reports
- Unsure if it has data

### How to Delete (Use with Caution)

<Steps>
  <Step title='Go to Disabled Vehicles'>
    Ensure vehicle is already disabled
  </Step>

<Step title='Verify No Trip Data'>
  Check that trip count is zero Confirm no historical data exists
</Step>

<Step title='Click Delete'>Click **⋮** → **Delete** on the vehicle</Step>

<Step title='Confirm Multiple Times'>
  Read warnings carefully Confirm you understand data will be lost
</Step>

  <Step title='Final Confirmation'>
    Type vehicle registration if prompted Click final delete button
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Impact on Reports

<Tabs>
  <Tab title='Historical Reports'>
    **Existing reports:** - Remain unchanged - Still show disabled vehicle data
    - Static snapshots preserved **Why?** Reports are historical records
  </Tab>

<Tab title='New Reports'>
  **Future reports:** - Can still include disabled vehicle's historical data -
  If filtering by date, disabled vehicle trips appear - Performance data
  available **Why?** Historical data is preserved
</Tab>

  <Tab title='Dashboard'>

    Historical periods include it - Vehicle Performance shows it as "inactive"

  </Tab>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion
    title='Can I disable multiple vehicles at once?'
    icon='layer-group'
  >
    **Answer:** Yes! In the Disable Vehicle dialog, check multiple boxes and
    confirm once.
  </Accordion>

<Accordion
  title='What happens to trips already assigned to disabled vehicle?'
  icon='route'
>
  **Answer:** Nothing - they remain intact Historical trips are preserved
  exactly as recorded
</Accordion>

<Accordion title='Can Staff disable vehicles?' icon='user'>
  **Answer:** No, Admin only Staff can request Admin to disable vehicles as
  needed
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title='How long should I keep disabled vehicles?' icon='clock'>
    **Recommendation:** - Minimum: 1 full financial year - Better: 3-5 years -
    Best: Indefinitely (they take minimal space) **Why?** Tax audits, legal
    requirements, historical analysis
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't find disabled vehicle" icon='magnifying-glass'>
    **Check:** 1. Are you in Disabled Vehicles page (not Add/Manage)? 2. Use
    search function 3. Clear any filters **If not found:** May not be disabled
    after all - check Add/Manage Vehicles
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title='Disable button not appearing' icon='ban'>
  **Cause:** Logged in as Staff, not Admin **Solution:** Request Admin to
  disable the vehicle
</Accordion>

  <Accordion
    title='Accidentally disabled wrong vehicle'
    icon='triangle-exclamation'
  >
    **Solution:** Immediately re-enable it 1. Click **Enable** on the vehicle 2.
    Confirm 3. Vehicle returns to active status **Impact:** Minimal if caught
    quickly
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
