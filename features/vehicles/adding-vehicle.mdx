## Quick Start

**To add a vehicle:**

1. Navigate to **Vehicles** → **Add/Manage Vehicles**
2. Click **Add Vehicle** button (top right)
3. Fill in the form: Registration, Type, Ownership
4. Click **Add vehicle**

<Info>
  **New to Travada?** See [Adding Vehicles (Getting
  Started)](/getting-started/adding-vehicles) for a beginner-friendly tutorial
  with screenshots.
</Info>

## Vehicle Registration Guidelines

**Format requirements:**

- Maximum 8 characters
- Letters and numbers only
- Automatically converts to uppercase
- No special characters (except spaces)

**Valid examples:**

- ✅ KBE 127H
- ✅ KDA 631Q
- ✅ KCG877T (space optional)
- ✅ ABC 1234

**Invalid examples:**

- ❌ KBE-127H (no hyphens)
- ❌ KBE_127H (no underscores)
- ❌ VERYLONGPLATE (too long)

<Warning>
  **Registration is permanent!** It cannot be edited after creation.
  Double-check before saving.
</Warning>

### Registration Best Practices

<Tabs>
  <Tab title='Standard Format'>
    **Use the official format:** - ABC 123X (3 letters, space, 3 digits, letter)
    - ABCD 123X (4 letters, space, 3 digits, letter) **Why?** Matches physical
    plates and government records
  </Tab>

  <Tab title='Consistency'>
    **Be consistent across all vehicles:** - If using spaces, always use spaces
    - If uppercase, always uppercase **Why?** Easier searching and sorting
  </Tab>

  <Tab title='Verification'>
    **Before clicking Add:** 1. Check against physical vehicle 2. Verify no
    typos 3. Confirm not already in system **Why?** Can't edit registration
    later
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Selecting Vehicle Type

Choose the type that best matches your vehicle's primary use:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title='Truck' icon='truck'>
    **Standard transport trucks** **Use for:** - General cargo transport -
    Medium to large loads - Multi-purpose hauling **Not for:** Specialized
    vehicles (use specific types)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Canter' icon='truck-pickup'>
    **Small delivery trucks** **Use for:** - Urban/local deliveries - Light
    cargo (up to 3 tons typically) - City route operations **Examples:**
    Mitsubishi Canter, Isuzu NKR, Toyota Dyna
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Dump Truck' icon='truck-ramp-box'>
    **Construction material hauling** **Use for:** - Sand, gravel, stones -
    Construction sites - Quarry to site transport **Also called:** Tipper
    trucks, tippers
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Reefer Truck' icon='temperature-low'>
    **Refrigerated transport** **Use for:** - Flowers for export - Fresh produce
    - Frozen goods - Temperature-sensitive cargo **Also called:** Cold chain
    trucks, freezer trucks
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Box Truck' icon='box-open'>
    **Dry goods rigid trucks** **Use for:** - Boxed goods - Palletized cargo -
    Retail deliveries - Non-perishable items
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Trailer Truck' icon='trailer'>
    **Container and long-haul** **Use for:** - 20ft/40ft containers - Port
    operations - Long-distance hauls - Import/export logistics **Also called:**
    Articulated trucks, semi-trucks
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Passenger Transport Vehicle' icon='van-shuttle'>
    **People transport** **Use for:** - School vans - Tour vans - City shuttle
    services - Employee transport **Examples:** Toyota Hiace, Nissan Caravan
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  **Not sure which type?** Choose the closest match. You can change it later in
  [Edit Vehicle](/features/vehicles/editing-vehicle).
</Tip>

## Ownership Status

Select whether the vehicle is owned by your company or hired/leased:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title='Owned'>
    **Select "Owned" when:** - Your company purchased the vehicle - It's a
    company asset - You have the title/ownership documents - Long-term part of
    your fleet **Financial implication:** - Appears as capital asset - All
    expenses are operational costs - Full control over usage
  </Tab>

  <Tab title='Hired'>
    **Select "Hired" when:** - Leased from leasing company - Rented
    short/long-term - Contract-based usage - Not owned by company **Financial
    implication:** - Rental/lease payments tracked separately - May have
    different cost structure - Contract may have end date
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  **Can change later:** Unlike registration, you can update ownership status if
  your situation changes (e.g., you purchase a hired vehicle).
</Info>

## After Adding a Vehicle

Once you click **Add vehicle**:

<Steps>
  <Step title='Success Confirmation'>
    You'll see a success message The form closes automatically
  </Step>

  <Step title='Vehicle Appears in List'>
    New vehicle shows in the vehicles table Sorted alphabetically by
    registration
  </Step>

  <Step title='Available for Trips'>
    Vehicle immediately appears in trip recording dropdowns Staff can now assign
    trips to this vehicle
  </Step>

  <Step title='Performance Tracking Begins'>
    System starts tracking: - Trip count - Income generated - Expenses incurred
    - Net profit/loss
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding Multiple Vehicles

For bulk vehicle entry:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title='One-by-One (Best for 1-10)'>
    **Process:** 1. Click **Add Vehicle** 2. Fill form 3. Click **Add vehicle**
    4. Form resets automatically 5. Repeat for next vehicle **Tips:** - Keep
    vehicle documents ready - Use Tab key to navigate fields - Verify each entry
    before submitting
  </Tab>

  <Tab title='Prepared List (Best for 10+)'>
    **Preparation:** 1. Create spreadsheet with: - Registration numbers -
    Vehicle types - Ownership status 2. Keep spreadsheet open while entering
    **Execution:** 1. Click **Add Vehicle** 2. Copy data from spreadsheet 3.
    Submit 4. Repeat quickly **Advantage:** Faster, fewer errors
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Registration already exists error" icon="copy">
    **Message:** "This registration number already exists"
    
    **Causes:**
    - Vehicle already in system
    - Typo that matches existing vehicle
    - Vehicle in disabled vehicles
    
    **Solutions:**
    1. Check Add/Manage Vehicles list
    2. Check Disabled Vehicles
    3. Verify you typed correctly
    4. If genuinely duplicate, choose different registration format
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Add Vehicle button missing" icon="ban">
    **Cause:** You're logged in as Staff, not Admin
    
    **Solution:** Contact your administrator to:
    - Add the vehicle for you
    - Or grant you Admin permissions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Form won't submit" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Causes:**
    - Missing required field
    - Registration too long (>8 characters)
    - Invalid characters in registration
    
    **Solutions:**
    1. Check all three fields are filled
    2. Verify registration length
    3. Remove special characters
    4. Try clicking dropdown options again
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="New vehicle not in trip dropdown" icon="eye-slash">
    **Cause:** Page hasn't refreshed
    
    **Solutions:**
    1. Refresh browser (F5 or Cmd+R)
    2. Navigate away and back to Expenditure
    3. Log out and log back in
    
    Vehicle should now appear
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Vehicle Type Decision Tree

Not sure which type to select? Follow this decision tree:

```
Does it carry people?
├─ Yes → Passenger Transport Vehicle
└─ No → Continue

Does it carry containers?
├─ Yes → Trailer Truck
└─ No → Continue

Is it refrigerated?
├─ Yes → Reefer Truck
└─ No → Continue

Does it dump/tip loads?
├─ Yes → Dump Truck
└─ No → Continue

Is it a small delivery truck (<3 tons)?
├─ Yes → Canter
└─ No → Continue

Does it have an enclosed box body?
├─ Yes → Box Truck
└─ No → Truck (general)
```
