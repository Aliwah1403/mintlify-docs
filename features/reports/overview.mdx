The Reports section is the central hub for every report generated in Travada. Whether it is a client invoice for a Trailer Truck job or an internal expense summary for your finance team, all reports are created, stored, and managed from here.

Reports in Travada are generated from real trip data — not manually typed. This means every figure in a report comes directly from what was recorded in Expenditure and Incomes, keeping your documents accurate and audit-ready.

## What You Can Do Here

- View all reports ever generated in your workspace
- Search for a specific report by name
- Open and share reports with clients or management
- Track how many times a report has been viewed
- Delete reports that are no longer needed
- Monitor reports that are expiring soon

## How Reports Are Generated

Reports are not created from within the Reports section itself. They are generated from two other sections:

| Report Type | Where It Is Generated |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Rate Report (client invoice) | Incomes section → Rate tab |
| Expense Report | Expenses section → All tab |

Once generated, the report appears here automatically and can be accessed at any time within its expiry window.

## The Three Report Views

### All Reports

Shows every report in your workspace — income reports, expense reports, and any other generated documents. Each entry shows:

- Report type
- Report name
- Date generated
- Created by (which user)
- Expiry period (default: 30 days)
- Number of views
- Actions: copy link, open, delete

### Recent Reports

A filtered view showing only the most recently generated reports. Useful when you need to quickly find something you just created without scrolling through the full list.

### Expiring Soon

Highlights reports that are approaching their 30-day expiry. Use this view to identify reports you need to save externally or reshare before they expire.

<Warning>
Reports expire after 30 days by default. If a client or your accounts team needs access to an older report, you will need to regenerate it from the Incomes or Expenses section using the same date range.
</Warning>

## Who Can Use This Section

Both Admin and Staff users can view and open existing reports.

<Note>
**🔒 Admin Only** - Generating new reports and deleting existing reports requires Admin privileges. Staff users can view and share reports but cannot create or delete them.
</Note>

## Common Workflows

**Sending a client invoice:**
Generate a Rate Report from the Incomes section, then come here to copy the shareable link and send it to your client.

**Monthly finance review:**
At the end of each month, generate an Expense Report and a Rate Report covering the full month. Both appear here and can be opened side by side for a complete P&L view.

**Audit preparation:**
Use the All Reports view to locate reports covering the period under review. Share links or open and print them as needed.

**Housekeeping:**
Check the Expiring Soon view at the start of each month. Regenerate any reports you still need before they disappear.

## Integration with Other Sections

- **Incomes** - Rate Reports and Detention Reports are generated here
- **Expenses** - Expense Reports are generated here
- **Expenditure** - The underlying trip data behind every report
