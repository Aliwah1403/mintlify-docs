An expense report gives you a clean, professional summary of all costs incurred over a specific period. Use it for month-end reconciliation, sharing with your accounts team, or preparing documentation for an audit.

<Note>
**🔒 Admin Only** - Only Admin users can generate expense reports. Staff users can view and filter expense data but cannot create reports.
</Note>

## When to Generate an Expense Report

- End of month, to close off your books
- Before a finance or management meeting
- When reconciling against bank statements
- When an accountant or auditor requests cost documentation
- After a particularly expensive period you want to document

## How to Generate an Expense Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Expenses section">
    From the main menu, click **Expenses**, then select the **All** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter to the period you need">
    Set your start and end dates. For example, 01 Jan 2026 - 31 Jan 2026 for a full January report. Confirm the total shown is what you expect.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Generate Report">
    Look for the Generate Report option above the expense table.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter a report name">
    Give the report a clear, descriptive name. Good examples:
    - "Fleet Expenses - January 2026"
    - "Q1 2026 Expenses - All Vehicles"
    - "December Costs - Management Review"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the report">
    Click to confirm. The report is instantly saved to your Reports section.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the Report Contains

The generated expense report includes:

- The report name and date generated
- The date range covered
- All expense entries within that period, organized by category
- Category totals (fuel, clerk fees, Milage fees, parking, repairs, extras)
- A grand total for the full period

The format is clean and professional — suitable for sharing with accountants, management, or external reviewers.

## Accessing the Report After Generation

Once generated, go to the **Reports** section from the main menu. Your new report appears at the top of the All Reports list and in the Recent Reports view.

From there you can:

- **Open** the report to view or print it
- **Copy the link** to share it with your team
- **Delete** it if it was generated in error

<Tip>
Name reports consistently so they are easy to find later. A format like "Category - Month Year" (e.g., "Fleet Expenses - March 2026") makes searching much faster when you have many reports.
</Tip>

## Report Expiry

All reports expire after **30 days** by default. After expiry, the report is no longer accessible via its link.

<Warning>
If you need to keep expense records beyond 30 days, open the report before it expires and save a copy externally (PDF, print, or screenshot). You can also regenerate the report at any time using the same date range.
</Warning>

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The total on the report looks too low">
    This usually means some trips were not recorded in Expenditure for that period, or expense fields were left blank during entry. Check the Expenditure section for the same date range and look for missing or incomplete trips.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I cannot see the Generate Report button">
    This feature is Admin only. If you are logged in as a Staff user, you will not see the option. Ask your Admin to generate the report, or have your account role updated.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I generated a report for the wrong date range">
    Delete the incorrect report from the Reports section and regenerate it with the correct dates. Deletion requires Admin access.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
