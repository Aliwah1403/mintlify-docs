## Overview

The Drivers section in Travada is where you manage all driver information and control which drivers are active in the system. Proper driver management ensures accurate trip tracking and enables performance analysis.

<img src="/images/drivers-page.png" alt="Drivers Management Page" />

## Why Driver Management Matters

Drivers are the human element of your logistics operations. Effective driver management enables:

- ✅ **Accurate trip assignment** - Know who drove each trip
- ✅ **Performance tracking** - Monitor trips per driver
- ✅ **Workload visibility** - See driver activity levels
- ✅ **Accountability** - Clear records of who was responsible
- ✅ **Payroll support** - Data for bonus/commission calculations

<Info>
  Every trip in Travada must be assigned to a driver. Without drivers in your
  system, you cannot record trips.
</Info>

## Driver Information Tracked

Each driver in Travada contains:

| Field              | Description            | Editable |
| ------------------ | ---------------------- | -------- |
| **Driver's Name**  | Full name              | ✅ Yes   |
| **Phone Number**   | Contact number         | ✅ Yes   |
| **Status**         | Active or Disabled     | ✅ Yes   |
| **Trip Count**     | Total trips driven     | Auto     |
| **Last Trip Date** | Most recent assignment | Auto     |

<Note>
  Unlike vehicles, both driver name and phone number can be edited after
  creation if information changes.
</Note>

<Steps>

  <Step title="Assign to Trips">

    Drivers](/features/drivers/disabling-drivers)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Re-enable if Needed">

  </Step>

## Common Workflows

### Daily Operations

**Normal day-to-day:**

- Drivers remain in system
- Used for trip assignments
- Activity tracked automatically
- No driver management needed

<Info>
  Most days you won't need to access driver management - drivers just work in
  the background!
</Info>

### Monthly Review

**End of month:**

1. Review trip counts per driver
2. Identify top performers
3. Check for inactive drivers
4. Verify all drivers have activity
5. Calculate bonuses/commissions if applicable

### Driver Changes

**When hiring new drivers:**

1. Navigate to Drivers → Add/Manage Drivers
2. Click **Add Driver**
3. Enter name and phone number
4. Driver immediately available for trips

**When drivers leave:**

1. Navigate to Drivers → Inactive Drivers
2. Click **+ Disable Driver**
3. Select driver(s) to disable
4. Confirm - removes from active use

## Driver vs. Vehicle Management

Understanding the differences:

| Aspect               | Drivers               | Vehicles                       |
| -------------------- | --------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **What**             | Human resources       | Physical assets                |
| **Typical Count**    | 5-50                  | 5-50                           |
| **Info Required**    | Name, phone           | Registration, type, ownership  |
| **Change Frequency** | Medium (months/years) | Low (years)                    |
| **Key Metric**       | Trip count            | Profitability                  |
| **Editing**          | Name & phone editable | Only type & ownership editable |
| **When Inactive**    | Disable               | Disable                        |

<Tip>
  Both drivers and vehicles should be disabled (not deleted) when no longer
  active to preserve historical data integrity.
</Tip>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many drivers can I add?" icon="users">
    **Answer:** Unlimited Travada supports teams of any size, from 1 driver to
    100+ **Note:** There's no separate per-driver charge (pricing is per
    vehicle)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I delete a driver?" icon="trash">
    **Not Recommended:** Deleting removes all trip history links **Better
    Option:** Disable the driver instead **Why?** Preserves trip assignments,
    maintains data integrity
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if two drivers have the same name?" icon="clone">
    **Solution:** Differentiate in the name field **Examples:** - "John Kamau
    (Senior)" and "John Kamau (Junior)" - "John K. - KBE 127H" (include vehicle
    if dedicated) - "John Kamau 001" and "John Kamau 002" **Also:** Phone
    numbers help distinguish them
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Staff add drivers?" icon="user">
    **Answer:** No, Admin only **Reason:** Personnel management is an
    administrative function **Workaround:** Staff can request Admin to add
    drivers as needed
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do drivers appear in reports?" icon="file-chart-column">
    **Answer:** Yes, trip reports include driver names **Used for:** - Verifying
    who drove specific trips - Client documentation - Internal performance
    tracking
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Difference from HR Systems

**Travada is NOT an HR system**

Travada tracks driver trip assignments, not:

- ❌ Employment contracts
- ❌ Salary details
- ❌ Leave management
- ❌ Performance reviews
- ❌ Disciplinary records

**Use Travada for:** Trip assignment and activity tracking

**Use separate HR system for:** Employment and personnel management
