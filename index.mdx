## What is Travada?

Travada is a logistics financial and vehicle performance management system designed to help transport and freight businesses track income, expenses, vehicle performance, and profitability with ease.

## Who is Travada For?

Travada is built for:

- **Small to mid-sized logistics companies** managing 5-50 vehicles
- **Trucking fleets** handling construction materials, refrigerated goods, dry cargo
- **Freight forwarders** dealing with container transport and detention fees
- **Transport businesses** tired of spreadsheets and manual financial tracking
