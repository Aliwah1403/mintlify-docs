## Overview

Travada has two user roles with different permission levels to help you manage access and maintain data security across your organization.

## User Role Types

  </Card>
  <Card

## Admin Role

### Capabilities

Admins have **full access** to all Travada features and can perform all actions, including:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Fleet & Driver Management" icon="truck">
    - Add, edit, and delete vehicles
    - Enable/disable vehicles
    - Add, edit, and delete drivers
    - Enable/disable drivers
    - View disabled vehicles and drivers
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Trip & Financial Management" icon="money-bill">
    - Record new trips and expenditures
    - Edit existing trip data
    - Delete trips
    - View all income and expense data
    - Apply fuel discounts (individual and bulk)
    - Remove fuel discounts
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Reporting & Analytics" icon="chart-bar">
    - Generate all types of reports (Rate, Expense, Detention)
    - Delete generated reports
    - View all dashboard analytics
    - Access vehicle performance metrics
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="System Settings" icon="gear">
    - Access organization settings
    - Manage notification preferences
    - Change appearance settings
    - Access account settings
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Who Should Be an Admin?

Assign the Admin role to:
- Business owners
- Operations managers
- Finance managers
- Senior staff who need full oversight

<Warning>
  **Important:** Only assign the Admin role to trusted team members, as they can delete critical data and access sensitive financial information.
</Warning>

## Staff Role

### Capabilities

Staff users have **limited access** focused on day-to-day operations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What Staff CAN Do" icon="check">
    - View the dashboard and analytics
    - Record new trips and expenditures
    - View income and expense data
    - View existing vehicles and drivers
    - View generated reports
    - Update their own account settings
    - Change appearance preferences
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What Staff CANNOT Do" icon="xmark">
    - Add, edit, or delete vehicles
    - Add, edit, or delete drivers
    - Enable/disable vehicles or drivers
    - Delete trips or financial data
    - Apply or remove fuel discounts
    - Generate new reports
    - Delete generated reports
    - Access organization settings
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Who Should Be Staff?

Assign the Staff role to:
- Trip recording clerks
- Data entry operators
- Junior staff who only need to input trip information
- Team members who should view but not modify fleet/driver data

## Admin-Only Features

Throughout this documentation, features that are **Admin-only** will be clearly marked with this badge:

<Note>
  ğŸ”’ **Admin Only** - This action requires Administrator permissions
</Note>

When you see this badge, remember that Staff users will not have access to perform that action.

## How to Identify Your Role

You can identify your current role by:

1. Looking at your user profile in the top right corner
2. Attempting to access restricted features (Admin-only features will be hidden or disabled for Staff users)
3. Checking with your system administrator

## Requesting Role Changes

If you need different permissions:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact Your Admin">
    Reach out to your organization's Travada administrator
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explain Your Needs">
    Describe which features you need access to and why
  </Step>
  <Step title="Admin Updates Role">
    Your administrator can change your role from the Organization Settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Best Practice:** Follow the principle of least privilege - users should only have the minimum permissions needed to perform their job functions.
</Tip>

## Security Recommendations

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Limit Admin Accounts" icon="shield-halved">
    Only create Admin accounts for users who absolutely need full system access
  </Card>
  <Card title="Regular Audits" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Periodically review who has Admin access and adjust as needed
  </Card>
  <Card title="Strong Passwords" icon="lock">
    Ensure all users, especially Admins, use strong, unique passwords
  </Card>
  <Card title="Role Training" icon="graduation-cap">
    Train users on their role's capabilities and limitations
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
