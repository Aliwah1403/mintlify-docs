## Overview

This guide will walk you through the essential first steps to start using Travada effectively. By the end, you'll have:

- ✅ Added your first vehicle
- ✅ Added your first driver
- ✅ Recorded your first trip
- ✅ Viewed your financial insights on the dashboard

<Note>
  **Estimated time:** 15 minutes
</Note>

## Step 1: Add Your First Vehicle

Vehicles are the foundation of your Travada system. You need to add vehicles before you can record trips.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Vehicles">
    Click **Vehicles** in the left sidebar, then click **Add/Manage Vehicles**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Vehicle">
    In the top right corner, click the **Add Vehicle** button

    ![Add Vehicle Button](/Screenshot2026-01-30at9.27.03am.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Vehicle Details">
    Fill in the required information:

    - **Vehicle Registration**: Enter the license plate number (e.g., KBE 127H)
    - **Vehicle Type**: Select from the dropdown (Truck, Canter, Dump Truck, Reefer Truck, Box Truck, Trailer Truck, or Passenger Transport Vehicle)
    - **Vehicle Ownership**: Choose either "Owned" or "Hired"

    ![Vehicle Form](/Screenshot2026-01-30at9.27.11am.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Vehicle">
    Click **Add Vehicle** to save

    Your vehicle will now appear in the vehicles list and be available for trip recording
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Best Practice:** Add all your active vehicles at once to save time later. You can add more vehicles anytime.
</Tip>

## Step 2: Add Your First Driver

Drivers must be added to the system before they can be assigned to trips.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Drivers">
    Click **Drivers** in the left sidebar, then click **Add/Manage Drivers**

    ![Navigate to Drivers]()
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Driver">
    Click the **Add Driver** button in the top right corner

    ![Add Driver Button](/Screenshot2026-01-30at9.27.31am-1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Driver Details">
    Fill in the driver information:

    - **Driver's Name**: Enter the full name (e.g., John Kamau)
    - **Driver's Phone Number**: Enter the contact number (e.g., 0765843567)

    ![Driver Form](/Screenshot2026-01-30at9.27.34am.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Driver">
    Click **Add Driver** to save

    The driver will now appear in your drivers list and be available for trip assignments
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Record Your First Trip

Now that you have vehicles and drivers set up, you can record your first trip expenditure.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Expenditure">
    Click **Expenditure** in the left sidebar

    ![Navigate to Expenditure](/images/expenditure-navigation.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Trip">
    Click the **\+ New Trip** button in the top right corner

    ![New Trip Button](/Screenshot2026-01-30at9.26.46am.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Basic Trip Information">
    The trip entry form has three pages. On the first page, enter:

    - **Trip Date**: Select the date the trip was taken
    - **Vehicle Registration**: Select the vehicle that made the trip
    - **Driver**: Select the driver who operated the vehicle
    - **Destination**: Enter the final destination (e.g., JAVA CBD, GM, PORTS)

    ![Trip Form Page 1](/Screenshot2026-01-30at9.26.54am.png)

    Click **Next** to continue
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Trip Costs">
    On the second page, enter all costs associated with the trip:

    **Income:**

    - **Rate**: The amount charged for the trip (e.g., KES 15,000)
    - **Detention Fee**: Any detention charges (for container delays)

    **Expenses:**

    - **Fuel Cost**: Amount spent on fuel
    - **Clerk Fee**: Payments to depot clerks (for container clearance)
    - **Mileage Fee**: Driver allowance for long-distance trips
    - **Parking Cost**: Any parking fees incurred
    - **Repair Cost**: Vehicle repair expenses during the trip
    - **Extra Cost**: Other miscellaneous costs (car wash, traffic fees, etc.)

    ![Trip Form Page 2](/images/trip-form-page2.png)

    <Info>
      **Note:** You don't need to fill in all fields. Only enter the costs that apply to this specific trip. Empty fields will default to KES 0.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the Trip">
    Click **Submit** to save the trip

    Your trip will now appear in the Expenditure table and all related pages (Income, Expenses, Fuel, etc.) will automatically update
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: View Your Dashboard

After recording your first trip, navigate to the **Dashboard** to see your financial insights.

![Dashboard Overview](/Screenshot2026-01-30at9.26.26am.png)

The dashboard shows:

- **Total Income** - Revenue from all recorded trips
- **Total Expense** - All costs incurred
- **Total Revenue** - Calculated profit/loss
- **Financial Overview Chart** - Visual comparison of income vs expenses
- **Top Destinations** - Highest revenue-generating routes
- **Expense Breakdown** - Pie chart showing where money is spent
- **Trip Completion Trend** - Daily trip activity by vehicle type
- **Vehicle Performance** - Top profitable vehicles and underperformers

<Check>
  **Congratulations!** You've successfully set up Travada and recorded your first trip. You can now continue adding more vehicles, drivers, and trips to build a complete picture of your business performance.
</Check>
